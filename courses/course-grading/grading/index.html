Grade Determination

We use a weighted grading system in this course. Although individual grade components will have points attached, the weight will be used to determine course grades. Grades will be maintained in Blackboard so you can determine your current grade at any time.

Source Weighted Percent
Class Membership 2%
Individual Preparation (top 4) 12%
Team Preparation (top 4) 6%
Homework (top 8) 20%
Exams (5 total) 60%

Final Grade

Your course grade is determined using two steps. First, your base grade (The letter A, B, C, D, or F without plus or minus) is determined using the table provided below.

Criteria Base Grade
≥ 90.0% A
≥ 80.0% B
≥ 70.0% C
≥ 60.0% D
< 60.0% F

Second, once your base grade has been determined, a plus/minus distinction may be added to your base grade. Note that I cannot award an “A+” or a “D-” since these grades do not exist at GVSU.
plus (+) A “plus” may be added to your base grade if you have earned at least 80% of the Application Quiz points and earned at least an 80% on Exam 5.
minus (-) A “minus” may be added to your base grade if you have earned less than 60% of the Application Quiz points or earned less than 60% on Exam 5.

Errors in Grading: While I strive for grading accuracy, mistakes in grading may still occur. If you feel a mistake in grading has occurred (i.e. grade entry error, calculation error), alert me within one week from when the grade in question was entered into Blackboard. After a week has passed following entry of a grade, I will assume that any entered grade is correct unless you communicate to me otherwise. As such, it is important that you take ownership of your grade throughout the semester!
